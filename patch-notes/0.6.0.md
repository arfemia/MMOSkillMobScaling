A content-authoring update built around mob families. Rarity tiers and a brand-new VARIANT overlay can now be locked to specific mob families, so you can build family-flavored elites, like a rare "Horrific" spider that stacks on top of whatever rarity it rolled and bites with its own venom, without any of it ever appearing on wolves or skeletons. Requires MMO Skill Tree 1.5.0+ and Ziggfreed's CommonLib 1.2.0+.

- **New: per-family gating.** Rarity tiers (and the new variants below) gain an optional `Families` block that whitelists and/or blacklists the mobs they may apply to, two ways you can mix: by native Hytale mob group (e.g. `Spiders`, `Undead`, `Trork`) or by role-name pattern (e.g. `Spider*`), case-insensitive. Deny always wins, and no block behaves exactly as before (eligible on every mob).
- **New: mob variant overlays.** A variant is a second, independent roll that STACKS on top of a mob's base rarity, so a spider can become a "Horrific Epic Spider": epic base times a horrific overlay. Each variant is family-gated, has its own spawn chance and difficulty band, its own stat multipliers (stacked on top of the rarity), and can grant its own exclusive affixes. It can also drop its own bonus loot (on top of the rarity's), carry a fallback body-tint that shows when the base mob has no rarity aura, and require certain base rarities (e.g. "only on epic and up"). At most one variant lands per mob, and the mob inspector shows it as its own coloured tag.
- **New: the horrific spider example.** Ships a spider-only `horrific` variant and its exclusive `venomous` affix as a worked example (delete the two files to remove it). Because `venomous` is granted only by the `horrific` variant, and `horrific` only rolls on spiders, the affix is automatically spider-exclusive with nothing extra to configure, the intended pattern for family-unique affixes.
- **Data-driven, as always.** The whole feature is authored in JSON assets (`Rarities/`, `Variants/`, `Affixes/`); a content pack can add its own family-locked tiers and variants or retune the shipped example per file.
